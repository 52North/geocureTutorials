Geocure REST API Demo Client

Using Leaflet, jQuery etc.

Click on the service you want to view:

    The map on the right will then display the service's data.

    The control in the top right hand corner allows to choose between basemaps and to add/remove map overlays from the WMS and WFS. Upon initialisation, the vector features from the WFS are loaded automatically while the image overlays from the WMS are turned off.

    Note: Some of the feature sets are bigger than 1MB, and one is bigger than 10MB, so it might take a while until everything appears (the green box will take the longest).
